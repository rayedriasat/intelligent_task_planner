Time Block Guidelines

One-time Blocks

  • • Use for specific dates and times
  • • Good for meetings or appointments
  • • Automatically removed after the date passes
  • • Can overlap with recurring blocks

Recurring Blocks

  • • Perfect for regular work schedules
  • • Repeats every week on the selected day
  • • Use the same start/end times each week
  • • Can be modified or deleted anytime

Best Practices

  • • Include buffer time between tasks
  • • Be realistic about your energy levels
  • • Consider your most productive hours
  • • Leave some unscheduled time for spontaneous work
